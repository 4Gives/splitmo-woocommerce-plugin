# SplitMo Checkout for WooCommerce

## Overview

Welcome to the Splitmo Payment Plugin! This plugin is designed to seamlessly integrate with WooCommerce, providing a reliable and secure payment gateway for your online store. With this plugin, you can enhance your customers' shopping experience by offering a variety of payment options while ensuring the safety of their transactions.

**Tags**: woocommerce, payment gateway, woocommerce extension, splitmo payment, payment, payment option, custom payment, 1-Click Checkout

## Features

- **Easy Integration**: Simple and straightforward integration with WooCommerce, allowing you to start accepting payments quickly.

- **Multiple Payment Options**: Support for a wide range of payment methods, including credit cards, digital wallets, and more.

- **Security**: Utilizes industry-standard security measures to ensure the safety of your customers' sensitive information during transactions.

- **Customization**: Easily customize the appearance and behavior of the payment gateway to match your brand and user experience.

- **Transaction Logs**: Keep track of all transactions with detailed logs for easy reconciliation and troubleshooting.

- **Responsive Design**: Ensures a seamless payment experience for your customers across various devices.

## Installation

To install the WooCommerce Payment Plugin, follow these simple steps:

1. Download the plugin zip file from the GitHub repository.

2. Log in to your WordPress admin panel.

3. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin**.

4. Upload the zip file you downloaded and click **Install Now**.

5. Once installed, activate the plugin.

6. Go to **WooCommerce > Settings > Payments** to configure the plugin settings.

7. Enable the payment methods you want to offer and enter the required credentials.

8. Save your settings, and you're ready to start accepting payments!

## Configuration

### General Settings

- **Enable/Disable**: Toggle the plugin on or off.
- **Title**: Set the title that customers will see during checkout.
- **Description**: Provide additional information about the payment method.

### Payment Methods

- Enable or disable specific payment methods (e.g., credit card, digital wallet).

### API Credentials

- Enter the necessary API credentials provided by your payment gateway provider.
